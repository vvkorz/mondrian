The Mondrian Art Generator

Generate your own Mondrian art with chosen partition complexity.

Simply input a positive real number in [0.001, 0.05]. The lower the complexity number, the less partitions are made.

You can choose between two syles: Mondrian art with lines and without lines.
For Mondrian art without lines, we dare you to input a higher complexity and see what happen (hint: 0.03 is a nice choice).
